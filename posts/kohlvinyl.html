Vinyl Records and Other Stuff at Kohls - Hammock Landing - West Melbourne, FL

Got dragged along to Kohls, and didn't find anything for myself. Did find some stuff of interest though... Such as this haphazard pile of vinyl records (seems to be the same source that Walmart gets their stuff from, same mix) and low-end Victrolas. Now I'm no vinyl snob, but stuff like this just seems kitschy. A different variety of Victrolas, stacked by the video games section. What records were left on the floor were haphazardly dumped in this cardboard box. Really just slobbish, for Kohls. These were all also priced a solid $5-$10 above Walmart prices. Some vinyl related fun in the ornaments display. Now, for where it all began for many...
