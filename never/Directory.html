Malls of Future Never

Malls come, malls go. They rise, they die, they get demolished and redeveloped. But some never even make it that far. These malls never left the planning board- if they were ever built, it was only partially, and they were almost never used for actual retail.

Remember to CTRL+F to search for a state (yes it's low tech, but it *works*).
